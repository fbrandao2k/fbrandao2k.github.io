Structures

  • Parametric Modeling

    I've been creating parametric modeling of Structural structures, such as bridges and steel structures. It allows the creation, comparison and optimization of multiple models. Example of softwares are CsiBridge, SAP2000, and Ansys. The programming languages I use more often for paramentric modeling are Vba, Python, and ADPL. Visual programming like Dynamo and Grasshopper are also very useful.

  • Structural Analysis

    Creation of meshed models with dynamic and static structural analysis.

  • Creation of FEM software

    Use of programming language Matlab for the creation of FEM software with a GUI to model, create the mesh, and do the structural analysis. The video was posted on youtube in 2018, and can be seen here.

  • Reinforced Concrete Detailing

    Modeling, structural analysis and detailing of structures such as buildings and retaining walls.
